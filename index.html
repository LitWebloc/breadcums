What are bread crumbs?

Bread crumbs, which are based on micro-markup from Google, are a special way to complement the snippet at the expense of the navigation chain, which demonstrates the place of the page in the site hierarchy. In fact, breadcrumbs replace the usual link in the search with the name of the sections and subsections in which the web page is located. The searcher gives the following example:

Why do we need bread crumbs?

The use of breadcrumbs can be due to several reasons:
  • the fragment extended by the navigation chain more actively attracts the attention of the search engine user, thereby increasing the likelihood of him following a link;
  • the presence of bread crumbs in the search results reduces the time to search for the necessary information through more conscious navigation through the site;
  • Complemented by a fragment of the navigation chain, according to individual optimizers, the page becomes more relevant when Google is issued.

These factors for many webmasters - a strong argument in favor of the introduction of the notorious bread crumbs in search results. And in order to bring them to your site, you can use our plugin.
